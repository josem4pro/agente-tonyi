# Get more help

If you encounter any problems while using, or have any feedback and suggestions, you can add Bohrium's official WeChat, or send an email to us, and we will reply as soon as possible.

You can click on the profile picture in the top right corner of the Bohrium page, and then click on "Contact Us" to view the official WeChat QR code and official email for Bohrium.
