# Bohrium®️ Space Station

This article will help you quickly understand and start using Bohrium.

## What is Bohrium[​](#what-is-bohrium "标题的直接链接")

Bohrium®️, developed by DP Technology, is a research cloud platform dedicated to enabling collaborative scientific research and industrial design at the microscale. You can explore scientific models such as DPA, Uni-Mol, Uni-RNA, Uni-Fold, and conduct research using these models on Bohrium. Bohrium offers educational tools such as Notebook platform, course platform, and competition platform. It features an AI research assistant based on large language models and provides research APP platform and developer tools, maximizing innovation efficiency and liberating research productivity. Bohrium also deeply optimizes algorithms and software for microscale scientific computing, such as first-principles calculations and molecular dynamics, providing massive high-performance computing power and efficient simulation environments.
